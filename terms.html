5. Bike Fit Analyzer

Bike Fit Analyzer measures joint angles from a video of you pedalling and grades them against ranges taken from bike-fitting research. It is a proof of concept, and there is no guarantee the numbers are right for your body. Camera angle, clothing, lighting and the pose model all introduce error, and the front/rear readings are explicitly experimental. See section 3 above — this is not a bike fit and not medical advice.

6. Aksara Bali

Aksara Bali converts Latin text into Balinese script in your browser. It is an educational tool offered free of charge to help people read, write and preserve Aksara Bali. Transliteration is imperfect: it may not match every scholarly or regional convention, and you should not rely on it for ceremonial, legal or published text without review by someone who knows the script.
